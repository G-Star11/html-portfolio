The Best Movies According to Gary

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Number 1. Pump up the Volume

Watched as a teenager. Very influential to be a rebel against the system and use technology to outwit to the establishment to bring about change for the good

Number 2. The Basketball Diaries

Watched in late teens. Influential to show the power of how good drugs can be at the ultimate cost - your life. Lesson - as great as drugs can be... Don't do drugs kids

Number 3. Trainspotting

A great "party" movie to show the joy of the different personalities of your friends and to love them for who they are

Number 4. The Power of One

Watched around 10 to 13 years old. Very impressionable movie on how corrupt the world is and how we must stand up to make the world a better place

Number 5. The Bourne Identity

Classic Mystery Action Thriller with the undertones of the secret government agencies and world controllers

Number 6. Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas

Number 7. Rainbow Bridge

Number 8. Naked Lunch

Number 9. Moana

Number 10. My own private Idaho

Trippy movie? Can't really remember it. But I loved River Phoenix movies. Struggles of growing up movie?
